Sport Information and Our Goals

  • What is TT Racing

    TT racing is the pincale of all things motorsport. It involes going to differnt towns nation wide and compeating in races. The race tracks are designed around the town streets, leading to extreamly close calls and sharp corners. Legally racing through the steet is every speed junkies dream. Reaching speeds of over 300 kmh while driving just centemeters from buildings gives one of the biggest adreniline rushes youll ever experience. Nothing compares to this amazing sport. Come try it out.

    Check out team roles! »

  • Brunswick TT racers goal

    At Brunswick TT Racers our main goal is to have fun. We provide access to an extream type of motorsports outlined beside. Through our team culture, our friendly community and passion for all things fast we have built a team to compete in TT races. Our team aims to keep everyone safe and involved at all times, regularly switching posions to ensure everyone has a go at everything. Our secondary goal for this season is to place in the top 3 teams in Austrlia, allowing us to compete overseas.
